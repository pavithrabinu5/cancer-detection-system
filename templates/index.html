Understanding Brain Tumors

Our AI system classifies brain MRI scans into four categories with high accuracy. Each classification provides crucial information for medical professionals.

Glioma

A tumor that originates from glial cells in the brain or spinal cord. Requires immediate medical attention and specialized treatment.

Meningioma

A tumor that forms on membranes covering the brain and spinal cord. Often benign but may require monitoring or treatment.

Pituitary Tumor

A growth in the pituitary gland affecting hormone production. Treatment varies based on size and hormone effects.

No Tumor

Normal brain tissue with no detectable tumor present. Regular check-ups still recommended for overall health.
